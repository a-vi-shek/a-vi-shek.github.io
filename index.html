Hi !
I am Avishek 

I am an experienced designer with a focus on prototyping and fabrication. Trained in traditional design discourse, my work with Fab Labs, research institutions and industry has cultivated a unique skill set for innovative solutions. My industry experience aids in my stakeholder engagements, while my academic background contributes to cross-disciplinary collaborations and my ability to analyze, decode and represent data. I am proficient in concept development, rapid prototyping, digital fabrication, and handcrafting. With this expertise, I can tread between ideation, planning and realization with ease.
